## About Our

Founded by Wesley (ฉลาม), a road accident survivor turned community advocate, Charoen Watthana represents hope and resilience in action. After experiencing firsthand the challenges of accessing timely care and support, Wesley dedicated his life to ensuring no one in his community would face such struggles alone.

### Our Vision

We envision a Thailand where:
- Help is available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week
- Young people have access to quality education and mentorship
- Public health services reach every corner of our communities
- No one faces crisis alone

### Why We're Different

Unlike traditional services that operate on limited schedules, our center will provide:
- **Round-the-clock availability** - Because emergencies don't wait for business hours
- **Integrated services** - Combining emergency care, education, and health services
- **Community-driven approach** - Led by locals who understand local needs
- **Sustainable model** - Designed for long-term community impact

### Our Impact Goals

Once fully operational, we aim to:
- Serve over 1,000 people monthly
- Provide educational support to 50+ youth annually
- Offer 24/7 emergency assistance
- Create a model for other communities across Thailand
